## 3. Key Products in Detail

### 3.1 Metal Crusher (金属破碎机)

Designed for crushing and processing various scrap metal materials to reduce transportation costs and increase recycling value.

#### Working Principle

1. Metal waste enters the crushing chamber via a conveyor system
2. A high-torque motor drives the rotor, whose hammers alternately strike the material
3. Material is torn and crushed between hammers and liner plates to specified particle sizes
4. Air circulation sorting system separates paint, rust, and plastic from metal
5. Magnetic separators further sort ferrous from non-ferrous materials

#### Key Features

- Heavy-duty steel plate housing with high-manganese steel lining
- Disc-type rotor with hammers on a bearing-supported horizontal shaft
- Circulating oil forced lubrication with cooling and temperature sensors
- High output, low energy consumption, uniform discharge
- Low noise, integrated dust removal for environmental compliance

#### Materials Processed

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Cans & Containers | Aluminum cans, tinplate, beverage cans, paint buckets |
| Sheet Metal | Thin iron sheets, color steel tiles, metal containers |
| Vehicles & Parts | Bicycles, motorcycle frames, auto parts, light vehicle shells |
| Scrap Metal | Iron slag, mixed scrap metal |

#### Industries Served

- Scrap metal recycling
- Metal smelting and refining
- Automotive recycling
- Construction & demolition waste processing

### 3.2 Scrap Steel Crusher (废钢破碎机)

Specialized equipment for crushing scrap steel into clean, high-quality recycled particles suitable for steel mills.

#### Working Principle

```
Motor → High-torque Rotor → Hammers strike scrap steel
       → Tear & compress against liner → Strip rust/paint
       → Magnetic separation → Clean steel output
```

#### Materials Processed

- Scrap steel, iron, copper, aluminum
- Color steel tiles, structural offcuts
- Paint buckets, iron drums, roll-up doors
- Light metal scrap, metal packaging
- End-of-life vehicles (with optional pre-shredding unit)

#### Advantages

- Enables premium-grade scrap for electric arc furnace charging
- Supports energy-saving, emission-reduction steel production
- Reduces reliance on mined mineral resources
- Lower transportation costs through densified output
- Optional hydraulic pre-crushing for large steel structures

## 5. Processing Line

A complete metal recycling production line typically follows this flow:

```
 Raw Material
      │
      ▼
 Conveyor Feeder ──→ Pre-Shredder (optional for large items)
      │
      ▼
 Main Crusher / Shredder
      │
      ▼
 Air Separation System ──→ Light Fractions (plastic, paint, fabric)
      │
      ▼
 Magnetic Separator ──→ Non-Ferrous Metals
      │
      ▼
 Clean Scrap Metal Output
```

### Optional Add-ons

- Hydraulic pusher for bulky materials
- Dust collection system
- Eddy current separator for non-ferrous metals
- Weighing and baling systems
